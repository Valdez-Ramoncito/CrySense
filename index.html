The Product

Google Translator for your crying infants.

Every parent knows the exhaustion of not knowing why their baby is crying. CrySense changes that. A small smart sound sensor listens to your baby and instantly sends you a notification with a care suggestion, whether they are hungry, uncomfortable, or just need to be held.

No expensive monitor required. CrySense pairs an affordable Arduino-based sensor with a free app, making smart infant care accessible to every family.

How It Works

01

The sensor listens

A compact smart sound sensor sits near your baby and automatically picks up their cry the moment it starts.

02

AI reads the cry

Our cry analyzer processes the audio in real time and identifies what your baby needs, from hunger and discomfort to fatigue.

03

You get the answer

Your phone receives an instant notification with a clear care suggestion so you can respond with confidence, wherever you are in the house.

Our Story

Why we built CrySense.

CrySense was founded by a team driven by one goal: to create reliable and meaningful solutions for modern childcare. The idea came from recognizing a common struggle many parents face, the exhausting trial-and-error phase of trying to understand a baby's cries. Seeing how this leads to stress, uncertainty, and sleepless nights inspired us to take action.

We wanted to build something that could truly support parents, reduce their anxiety, and help them respond to their baby's needs with confidence. CrySense is that something.

Purpose

CrySense exists to support parents by reducing the stress and uncertainty of understanding their baby's cries, empowering guardians with reliable insights to respond with confidence and provide better care.

Mission

To provide a high-integrity technological bridge between guardians and infants through intelligent audio diagnostics and precision IoT engineering, delivering real-time insights that replace trial-and-error with informed, immediate action.

Vision

To be the premier pioneer of AI-driven infant communication solutions in the ASEAN region, setting the industry benchmark for smart childcare innovation by 2030.
